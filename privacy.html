Privacy Policy

This page explains what information RSJ Works collects through the website, why it is used and how it is handled.

Who We Are

RSJ Works

RSJ Works is a fabrication and commercial interiors business. For privacy queries, you can contact us at hello@rsj.works.

Office address: 1 Bishops Square, London E1 6AD, United Kingdom.

What We Collect

Information submitted through the website.

If you use the contact form, RSJ Works may receive your name, email address, company name and any project details you choose to send.

We may also receive normal technical information such as the time of submission and basic delivery records from the form processing service.

Why We Use It

To respond to enquiries and discuss work.

We use this information to review enquiries, reply to messages, discuss possible work and maintain a record of project correspondence where needed.

Our lawful basis for this is usually legitimate interests, and in some cases steps taken at your request before entering into a contract.

Third Parties

Form processing.

The contact form on this website is processed by FormSubmit. Messages sent through the form are forwarded to hello@rsj.works.

You should avoid sending sensitive personal data through the website unless it is genuinely needed for the enquiry.

Retention

How long information is kept.

Enquiry information is kept only for as long as reasonably needed to respond, assess work, manage ongoing discussions or keep an appropriate business record.

Your Rights

UK GDPR and EU GDPR rights.

Depending on your location and the circumstances, you may have rights to access, correct, erase or restrict the use of your personal data, or to object to certain processing.

To make a request, contact hello@rsj.works. If you are in the United Kingdom, you also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office.
