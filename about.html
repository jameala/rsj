About RSJ Works

RSJ Works was founded by three school friends who share a simple view: things should be made properly.

The company focuses on practical problems, careful design and dependable fabrication.

We work across the full life of a project: concept, design development, fabrication, delivery and installation.

With a primary base in London and satellite operations in Maine, RSJ Works supports work on both sides of the Atlantic.

  • Commercial fabrication and installation
  • Design development that can be built properly
  • Reliable performance in daily use

Approach

A practical design-build approach from first discussion to handover.

Dependability

Projects are shaped around real site conditions, daily use and sound long-term performance.

Craftsmanship

Materials, details and finishes are handled with care and consistency.

Practical Design Thinking

Decisions are guided by buildability, maintenance, installation logistics and use on site.
