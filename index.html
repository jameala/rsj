Process

From first idea to final installation.

Step 1

Consultation

We review the site, how the space will be used and the limits that matter.

Step 2

Design

Layouts and details are resolved with buildability, maintenance and use in mind.

Step 3

Fabrication

Components are made with close attention to materials, detailing and finish.

Step 4

Delivery

Delivery is planned carefully so installation can proceed without unnecessary delay.

Step 5

Installation

The finished work is installed, checked and handed over ready for service.

Step 6

Handover

Final details are reviewed on site so the completed work is ready to use from day one.
